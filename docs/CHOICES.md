# Engineering Tradeoffs

## Why YOLOv8n Instead of YOLOv8x?

YOLOv8n provides significantly faster inference while maintaining acceptable accuracy for store analytics.

## Why SQLite Instead of PostgreSQL?

Reduced setup complexity and faster local deployment.

## Why Rule-Based Anomalies?

Given the limited time and dataset size, rule-based detection provides explainable and reliable results.

## Why Zone Analytics?

Zone popularity directly maps to business value and merchandising decisions.
